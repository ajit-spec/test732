India is the world's largest democracy and the seventh-largest country by land mass.
It is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse languages, and ancient civilization spanning over 5,000 years.
The country is home to various religions including Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Sikhism, Buddhism, and Jainism.
India has made significant contributions to science, mathematics, literature, and arts throughout its history.
The country is also a major hub for information technology services and outsourcing.
Agriculture remains a significant sector, employing about half of India's workforce.
The Indian economy is one of the fastest-growing major economies in the world.
The country is also renowned for its vibrant film industry, known as Bollywood, which produces numerous movies annually.
